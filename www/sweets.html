--- Pricing ---
Cake pricing listed is for a signature C. Marie's frosted butter cream cake. Custom cake designs are welcomed and will be priced according to design materials and decorating time. Please send us a picture (of envisioned designs, etc.) with the number of servings needed for an accurate quote-- we will be happy to customize your cake to fit your budget and style!


Tiered Cakes

All tiered cakes start at a $125 and serve 35 people at a minimum. Buttercream starts at $3.50 per slice and fondant starts at $4.00 for a simple design, any fondant characters, logos, elaborate fondant work, sugar flowers, or any other labor-intensive design are priced specifically to the client's order. Cream Cheese, ganache, german chocolate, smores and brandied cherry filling is an extra $.25 per serving. A credit card is required to place a custom cake order. C. Marie's bakes each cake fresh to order with you in mind. Please give us a week's notice for your order, but if time gets away, do not hesitate to call us- we will do our best to make cake magic happen!
