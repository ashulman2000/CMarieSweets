--- Custom Wedding Cakes ---

Cakes specially tailored just for you.
Schedule a complimentary 45 minute consultation to discuss the style and tone of your event. During the appointment, we’ll collaborate on your vision and design. Please bring any inspirational materials, such as your invitation or pictures. C.Marie's tasting times are as follows:
Wednesday thru Friday between 10am-2pm, and Saturdays at 10am-11am-or 12pm.
Saturday appointments book out a week in advance, so contact us today!

Sweet occasions.
C.Marie’s also offers desert bars, sweets tables, and custom Grooms cakes. These additions are a great way to make you and your guests feel special. For pricing, please contact for an estimate.













Purchase the cake of your dreams.
Wedding cakes vary in price depending on the decor, flavor, and quantity of servings. Buttercream cakes start at $3.75 per slice and fondant starts at $4.50. With the purchase of a Bride's cake, Groom's cakes start at $3.25 per serving. All custom designs and fondant-work are additional. Simply iced cupcakes are $2.75 each- any custom toppers or designs are additional. Wedding cakes are subject to a non-refundable deposit of half the total cost.
